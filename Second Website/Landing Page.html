3 Tips For Productivity

Tip 1: Wake up early

Sleeping daily can affect your mental health in many ways. It can help you work more accurately, and stay productive throughout the day. Nothing is better than having a sleep schedule. You may say: I alwasy make sure to sleep up to 9 hours a day, but don't have a strict schedule, but don't feel good about myself. The point here is to show you that even if you had great amount of sleep, that doesn't mean that you will feel good about it. Studies shown that people who sleep between 7 and 9 hours are less productive, comparing to the people who sleep between the 10 PM and 7 AM. It doesn't matter if you can all those amount of sleep, if you don't have a strict sleep schedule that you're willing to stick to.

Tip 2: Exercice Daily

Working out daily can help you endure hard tasks and challenges. You will make sure you have 100% ability to concentrate throughout the day.Working out in the mornings has also shown some benefits, from increased energy to low stress levels. There's no doubt that working out in the morning can let you feel good about yourself. But what about your school or job? What about your family and kids. How do you manage time? This is were the idea of flexible workout routine comes to: Unlike sleep, you can easily do workouts througout the day at any time. If the morning is a good fit for you, that workout in the morning. If the evening is great, do it. The important thing is to show up, and not make a full workout. If one day you feel like you don't want to workout, it's okay. Go to the gym (or your house), and show up. Do Do some streches, and maybe any light exercise, and that's it.

Tip 3: Meditate

Meditation was always something peaceful, and it will always be. Unlike many celebrities, Katy Perry and Oprah Winfrey believes that meditation is the practice of silencing the thoughts in your mind to achieve awareness. And you should do it too. Maybe you choose to workout only. Maybe meditation only. Maybe both. But what's important is remembering that you have to maintain the peacful and calm mind to live a happy life. You have to know when to sprint and when to rest. Burnout is the result of not regulary checking your mental state. Hard work is important, but doing it in a conscious way is important to. We should not forget that we are humans, and overworking can make us make some mistakes.
